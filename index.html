How it works
From rehearsal to show day.
Etch fits into your existing workflow. No training manuals, no onboarding calls.

Create a session

Set up your show, import or build a setlist, and invite your crew. Everyone joins with their name, department, and colour.

Log notes in real time

During rehearsal or tech, drop a note at the current timecode. Tag a department, describe the issue, and move on. It takes two seconds.

Review and resolve

After the run, switch to the timeline or detail view. Filter by department, walk through each note, and mark them fixed or ignored.
